## 1. What this phase delivers

Phase 3 turns Manifest from a smart dispatch and load management platform into a full operations backbone. Three modules ship in this phase: Compliance tracking for DOT and non-DOT carriers, fleet management for vehicles of any class, and a CRM for managing customers, brokers, lanes, and rate agreements. These modules share data with the intelligence layer from Phase 2 so Marie can surface compliance deadlines, maintenance predictions, and customer insights proactively.

This phase covers every carrier type in scope: medical transport vans, box trucks, hotshots, straight trucks, and Class 8 semis. DOT-regulated and non-DOT operations share the same interface with compliance modules toggled per carrier profile.

## 2. Compliance module

### 2.1 Overview

The compliance module tracks every regulatory obligation a carrier faces and alerts before deadlines hit. It adapts based on carrier profile: a non-DOT medical van operator sees insurance and vehicle inspection reminders while a Class 8 interstate carrier sees the full DOT compliance suite.

### 2.3 Edge functions

**compliance-scanner** (pg_cron: daily at 6 AM)
- Scans all compliance_items where status != 'completed' and status != 'not_applicable'
- Calculates days until due_date
- Creates compliance_alerts at each threshold in alert_days_before
- Updates compliance_item status: due_soon (<=30 days), overdue (past due)
- Triggers push notification to assigned user and org admins
- For recurring items where completed_date is set: auto-generates next occurrence based on recurrence_rule

**ifta-aggregator** (pg_cron: weekly on Sunday)
- Pulls GPS/ELD data from telematics integrations (Phase 2 data feeds)
- Cross-references fuel card transactions
- Populates ifta_records per vehicle per jurisdiction
- Flags discrepancies between GPS miles and fuel card miles > 5% variance

### 2.6 Marie integration

Marie monitors compliance_alerts via Supabase Realtime. When a critical alert fires (overdue or expiring within 7 days), Marie proactively messages the relevant user:

- "Your annual DOT inspection for Unit 2847 expired 3 days ago. Want me to find inspection shops near your current location?"
- "Driver Mike Johnson's medical card expires in 6 days. He needs to schedule a physical. Want me to send him a reminder?"

Marie can also answer compliance questions using the Claude API with compliance context injected:
- "When is my IFTA filing due?" -> Checks ifta_records, returns next quarter deadline
- "Is my fleet inspection-ready?" -> Scans inspections table, returns pass/fail summary

### 3.3 Edge functions

**maintenance-monitor** (pg_cron: daily at 5 AM)
- For each active vehicle: checks current odometer and date against all active maintenance_schedules
- Calculates next due maintenance based on last maintenance_record of that type
- Creates compliance_items (category='scheduled_service') when maintenance is due within alert threshold
- Updates vehicle status to 'in_shop' when date_out is null on any maintenance_record

**fuel-card-sync** (pg_cron: every 6 hours)
- Pulls recent transactions from integrated fuel card APIs (Comdata, EFS, WEX) via Composio
- Deduplicates against existing fuel_transactions
- Updates vehicle odometer from fuel card readings
- Flags anomalies: fuel purchases > 150% of tank capacity, impossible distance between fillups

## 4. CRM module

### 4.1 Overview

The CRM tracks every business relationship in the operation: customers who ship freight, brokers who post loads, vendors who service vehicles, and lanes that get run regularly. This is not a generic sales CRM. It is built for logistics relationships where the core objects are customers, brokers, lanes, and rate agreements.

- Recalculates crm_lanes aggregate fields: avg_rate_per_mile, total_runs, last_run_date

## 5. Cross-module integration points

These three modules do not operate in silos. Key data flows:

- When a load completes (Phase 1 loads table), CRM auto-updates company revenue, lane stats, and logs a system activity
- When a vehicle logs a DOT inspection (inspections table), compliance_items auto-complete and the next annual inspection is scheduled
- When a driver's CDL expiry passes (driver_qualifications), the driver is flagged in both compliance and fleet, and their active loads are alerted
- When Marie processes a new load request, she checks CRM for company payment history and compliance for driver/vehicle eligibility before accepting
- Fuel transactions feed into both fleet cost-per-mile and IFTA compliance calculations
